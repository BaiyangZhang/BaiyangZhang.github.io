Let $z=\rho e^{i\phi}$, then $dz=d\rho e^{i\phi}+i\rho e^{i\phi}d\phi$, hence 
$$
dz\wedge dz^\ast =(d\rho e^{i\phi}+i\rho e^{i\phi}d\phi)\wedge (d\rho e^{-i\phi}-i\rho e^{-i\phi}d\phi) = 2i\rho d\phi \wedge d\rho
$$
where we have used $da\wedge db=-db \wedge da$. The integrand reads
$$
e^{-az z^\ast } = e^{-a\rho^{2}},
$$
hence the integral is 
$$
\int dz\wedge dz^\ast  \,  e^{-azz^\ast } = 2i\int_ {0}^{2\pi} d\phi \int_ {0}^{\infty} d\rho \, \rho e^{-a\rho^{2}} =   2i\int_ {0}^{2\pi} d\phi\frac{ 1}{2a}  = \frac{2\pi i}{a.}
$$
